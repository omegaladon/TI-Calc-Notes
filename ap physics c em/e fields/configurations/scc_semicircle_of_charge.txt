
dEy = dEcos(O)
    = kdq/R^2 * cos(O)
    = kndl/R^2 * cos(O)
    = knRdO/R^2 * cos(O)
    = kncos(O)dO/R
Ey  = (-PI/2)(int)(PI/2) dEy
    = (-PI/2)(int)(PI/2) kncos(O)dO/R
    = kn/R (-PI/2)(int)(PI/2) cos(O)dO
    = kn/R [sin(PI/2) - sin(-PI/2)]
    = kn/R [1 - (-1)]
    = 2kn/R

where
R = radius
